Glance est un client mobile et desktop clair et lisible pour vos analytics Umami et Plausible. Tous vos sites, d'un simple coup d'œil — sans serveur tiers, sans pub, sans traçage.

VOS SITES, RÉUNIS
• Ajoutez vos comptes Umami et Plausible : Glance liste tous vos sites automatiquement.

• Vos identifiants restent chiffrés sur votre appareil.

Glance ne collecte, ne vend et ne partage aucune donnée. Il affiche simplement les statistiques que vos propres outils produisent déjà.

Glance is a clean, readable mobile and desktop client for your Umami and Plausible analytics. All your sites at a glance — no third‑party server, no ads, no tracking.

ALL YOUR SITES, TOGETHER
• Add your Umami and Plausible accounts: Glance lists every site automatically.
• A single dashboard summing visitors, page views and visits across all your sites.
• Incremental loading: each site shows up as soon as its data arrives.

REAL TIME
• Live visitor counts for each site, refreshing on their own.
• A dedicated "Live" view, sorted by activity.

DETAIL WHEN YOU WANT IT
• Per site: visitors, page views, visits, bounce rate, average duration.
• Smoothed chart over the period you pick (today, 24 h, 7 d, 30 d, 12 months, or custom).
• Top pages, traffic sources, countries, events.

GROUPS TO STAY ORGANIZED
• Group your sites ("Games", "Clients", "Personal"…) and read their stats on their own.
• A group is a reading scope: totals and charts only count its sites.

WIDGETS
• Put your totals or a specific site on your home screen.

ONE‑SCAN TRANSFER
• Move your whole setup to another device via an encrypted QR code — nothing to retype.

RESPECTFUL AND OPEN
• No middle server: Glance talks straight to your Umami and Plausible instances.
• Your credentials stay encrypted on your device.
• 100% free and open source — a project by the My‑Monkey collective.

Glance collects, sells and shares no data. It simply displays the statistics your own tools already produce.

## Data safety (déclaration Google Play)
- **Collecte de données** : AUCUNE. L'app ne collecte ni ne transmet de données personnelles à My-Monkey ni à un tiers.
- Les identifiants Umami/Plausible saisis par l'utilisateur sont **stockés chiffrés sur l'appareil** (Keystore/EncryptedSharedPreferences), jamais envoyés ailleurs qu'à l'instance analytics de l'utilisateur.
- Trafic **chiffré** (HTTPS) vers les instances de l'utilisateur.
- Pas de publicité, pas d'identifiants publicitaires, pas de SDK d'analytics tiers dans l'app.
